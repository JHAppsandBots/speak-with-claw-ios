# Privacy Policy — Speak with Claw

**Last updated:** February 23, 2026

## Summary

Speak with Claw does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All communication happens directly between your iPhone and your own server.

---

## Data Collection

**We collect nothing.** No analytics, no tracking, no crash reporting, no third-party SDKs.

## Microphone Access

The app uses your microphone solely to capture your voice for sending to your own relay server. Audio is never stored on the device beyond the moment of transmission.

## Data Transmission

All data (voice, text) is sent exclusively to the server URL you configure in the app settings. This is your own server — we have no access to it.

## Chat History

Chat history is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. It never leaves your device except when you explicitly send a message to your server.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
