Speak with OpenClaw turns your iPhone into a voice interface for your AI bots running on OpenClaw — fully hands-free, no button holding, no hotword required.

Just talk. The app automatically detects when you start and stop speaking, sends your voice to your relay server, and your bot responds with audio. Real back-and-forth conversation, like a phone call.

Features:
• Hands-free VAD mode — automatic speech detection
• Multi-bot support — switch between different bots
• Chat history per bot
• 100% private — connects only to your own server, no cloud, no tracking

Requirements:
• iPhone with iOS 17+
• Mac running OpenClaw with voice relay server
• Local network or Tailscale connection

This app is free and open source (MIT). 

Note: This is an early-stage release. Core features work well, but some functions are still rough around the edges or not yet fully implemented. Feedback and contributions welcome on GitHub.
